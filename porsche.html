LONG STORY SHORT

Users spent an average of 47 minutes configuring their Porsche but 64% abandoned without completing. The overwhelming number of customization options created analysis paralysis. I designed a Design Recommendations feature that provides curated expert configurations as inspiring starting points while preserving full customization freedom.

THE CHALLENGE

How might we reduce decision fatigue in the configurator while maintaining the premium brand experience and complete customization freedom?

THE SOLUTION

Expert-curated configurations with design rationale that serve as inspiration, not prescription, allowing users to start confident and customize from there.

78%

Completion rate with recommendations (up from 36%)

22 min

Average configuration time (down from 47 minutes)

54%

Increase in user confidence scores

MARKET RESEARCH

I analyzed luxury automotive configurators from BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, and Tesla to understand how competitors approached configuration guidance.

WHAT COMPETITORS DO

  • High quality 3D visualization
  • Some preset configurations
  • Clear pricing transparency
  • Save and share capabilities

THE GAP

  • Presets feel generic without storytelling
  • No rationale for why combinations work
  • Lack of personalization or guidance
  • Premium upsells vs helpful starting points

SPEAKING TO USERS

OPPORTUNITIES

Users don't want to be told what to choose, but they desperately want guidance and validation. Expert recommendations should serve as an inspiring starting point while preserving complete customization freedom.

KEY INSIGHTS

  • Most successful configs started with clear vision or example
  • Users valued design rationale behind combinations
  • Dealership specialists provided confidence through guidance
  • Abandonment correlated with uncertainty

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

  • Inspiration, not prescription
  • Education builds confidence
  • Seamless transition to customization
  • Premium brand experience throughout

EARLY CONCEPT

The Design Recommendations feature introduces expert-curated configurations at the entry point of the configuration journey. Each recommendation includes design rationale explaining why elements work together harmoniously.

Curated Cards

Premium cards with color-matched backgrounds and clear naming conventions

Design Rationale

Expert commentary explaining why certain combinations work together

Dual Pathways

Start with design or explore details first

TESTING & ITERATION

Tested with 24 participants across two phases, including Porsche owners and prospective customers.

PHASE 1 FINDINGS

  • 92% engaged with recommendations first
  • Color-matched backgrounds called "premium"
  • Curated badge increased trust
  • Users wanted more options

PHASE 2 RESULTS

  • Completion rate: 36% → 78%
  • Configuration time: 47 min → 22 min
  • Confidence scores up 54%
  • 89% would use in real scenario

FINAL DESIGN

WHAT WORKED

  • Framing as inspiration vs prescription
  • Color-matched immersive backgrounds
  • Design rationale builds confidence
  • Seamless transition to customization

KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Guidance through complexity beats simplification
  • Education and transparency build trust
  • Premium comes from attention to detail
  • Preserve user freedom while reducing paralysis
