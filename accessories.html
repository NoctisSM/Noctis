LONG STORY SHORT

With the COVID-19 pandemic accelerating e-commerce adoption, I redesigned the Invisalign accessories website to create a seamless online shopping experience. The challenge was reducing friction in the ordering process while maintaining trust in purchasing medical products online.

THE CHALLENGE

Customers faced long in-store queues and limited shopping hours. With pandemic safety concerns, there was urgent need for a convenient online ordering experience.

THE SOLUTION

A streamlined e-commerce platform with intuitive navigation, multiple payment options, and clear product information that builds trust.

MARKET RESEARCH

I analyzed leading e-commerce platforms including Bloomingdale's, Asos, and Nike to understand best practices and identify opportunities for improvement.

KEY FINDINGS

  • E-commerce was one of the fastest-growing categories in 2020
  • Contactless shopping demand increased during pandemic
  • Consumers prefer ordering from retailer apps over in-person
  • Time-saving services encourage higher spending

OPPORTUNITIES

  • Simple, intuitive navigation with clear labels
  • Multiple payment options beyond credit cards
  • Accessible design with proper contrast ratios
  • Quick-add functionality for repeat orders

SPEAKING TO USERS

I conducted surveys with 27 participants and analyzed user reviews from App Stores to understand pain points and priorities when shopping online.

OPPORTUNITIES

Users need a fast, convenient way to order medical accessories online without the frustration of long queues and limited payment options. The solution must build trust through clear information while streamlining the purchase process.

USER NEEDS

  • Multiple payment methods (not just credit cards)
  • Ability to reorder favorite products quickly
  • Shipping notifications and tracking
  • Quick-add to cart functionality
  • Clear, accessible product information

DESIGN PRINCIPLES

  • Align with Invisalign brand guidelines
  • Ensure AA accessibility standards
  • Create intuitive navigation patterns
  • Build trust through transparency
  • Minimize friction in checkout flow

DESIGN SYSTEM

Using Invisalign's brand guidelines, I created a comprehensive UI style guide with the HSL color model for precise tints and shadows, and a typography scale to maintain hierarchy throughout the project.

TESTING & ITERATION

I tested the prototype with 5 users, focusing on the ordering process from adding items to cart through checkout completion.

INITIAL FINDINGS

  • 60% couldn't complete purchase initially
  • All users successfully added items to cart
  • Coupon codes worked intuitively
  • Main issue: credit card-only payment

IMPROVEMENTS MADE

  • Added PayPal and Stripe options
  • Improved payment method clarity
  • Enhanced accessibility (AA standards)
  • Streamlined checkout flow

FINAL DESIGN

IMPACT & LEARNINGS

WHAT WORKED

  • User testing revealed critical payment issues
  • Colorful brand guidelines created friendly feel
  • Iterative design process improved outcomes
  • Accessibility checks ensured inclusivity

KEY TAKEAWAYS

  • Always validate assumptions with real users
  • Payment options significantly impact conversion
  • Brand consistency builds trust
  • Accessibility is essential, not optional
