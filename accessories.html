Invisalign E-Commerce Website (2020)

Challenge
While Invisalign treatment changed how people straightened their teeth, the online shopping experience did not always match that same simplicity. On shop.invisalign.com, customers were often unsure which products were right for them. Some were still in treatment, others had just finished, and many were maintaining their new smile. With so many accessories, cleaning systems, and retainer options available, the lack of clear guidance made it easy for users to hesitate or leave without completing their purchase. The challenge was to design an online experience that felt as seamless, modern, and reassuring as the Invisalign brand itself.

Solution
To make the journey more intuitive, I explored ways to bring guided discovery into the shopping process. The goal was to help users quickly understand what they needed, why they needed it, and how it fit into their personal smile stage. The updated concept for the store focused on three key ideas:

Creating personalized starting points like “In treatment,” “Just finished,” and “Keeping it straight,” each leading to curated product selections.

Using clear visual storytelling for every product, showing what it does and how it supports daily care.

Streamlining the purchasing flow with simpler navigation, clearer compatibility information, and a smoother checkout.

The new structure offered both guidance and flexibility. It helped users feel supported without losing their sense of control or independence during the buying process.

Impact
The refreshed experience made the Invisalign Shop feel less like a generic store and more like a trusted companion for every stage of the smile journey. Early testing and feedback revealed several positive outcomes:

Customers found the right products faster and completed more purchases.

The interface felt intuitive and premium, consistent with Invisalign’s overall brand experience.

Returning customer rates improved, especially among those buying retainers and cleaning accessories.

By rethinking how people shop for Invisalign products, the new e-commerce flow created a smoother and more personal experience. It allowed customers to feel confident, cared for, and connected from discovery to delivery.
