Long Story Short

I redesigned the patient experience for 12 million Invisalign users worldwide, creating a centralized dashboard that transformed treatment engagement.

I worked as a product designer on the Invisalign Patient Dashboard, improving the digital experience for millions of orthodontic patients. Align Technology taught me to design healthcare products that balance medical precision with empathetic, human-centered experiences.

I conducted extensive user research with patients and orthodontists, led cross-functional design workshops, sought feedback from clinical stakeholders, and presented my final designs to the SVP of the Global Invisalign Brand. The dashboard was successfully launched in 2021. I owned three key initiatives:

Led the dashboard redesign, resulting in a 35% increase in patient engagement and improved treatment adherence across 12 million active users.

Designed a personalized progress tracking system used daily by patients, collaborating with clinical teams and engineering partners to ensure medical accuracy.

Built a comprehensive resource library by interviewing 80+ patients and 40+ orthodontists to address common questions, resulting in a 28% reduction in support calls.

Challenge

Staying connected throughout the journey

Once treatment began, many Invisalign patients found it difficult to stay connected to their progress. They received aligners, followed instructions, and attended appointments, but often lacked a clear sense of how far they had come or what was next.

Without an easy way to visualize progress, track wear time, or communicate with their doctor, motivation tended to fade over time. The existing experience felt fragmented. Patients had to rely on multiple emails, manual reminders, or third-party apps to stay on track.

The challenge was to design a central space that could keep patients engaged and informed throughout their smile journey.

Solution

A personalized hub for every stage

The idea behind the new Invisalign Patient Dashboard was to create a single, personalized hub that brought clarity and encouragement to every stage of treatment. The dashboard became a digital companion, helping patients see their journey.

The new experience allowed patients to view their treatment progress at a glance, including completed and upcoming aligner stages, receive personalized reminders for aligner changes and check-ins, access educational tips and support resources tailored to their phase of treatment, and stay connected with their doctor through messages and virtual updates when needed.

The layout balanced structure and freedom. It guided patients through their treatment without ever feeling restrictive, making the process feel transparent, motivating, and personal.

Impact

An interactive and encouraging journey

The dashboard transformed how patients experienced Invisalign treatment. What once felt like a hidden process became an interactive and encouraging journey. Post-launch analytics and patient feedback showed measurable improvements across key metrics.

Patient engagement with the platform increased by 35%, with users checking their progress an average of 4 times per week. Treatment adherence improved significantly, with a 22% reduction in delayed aligner changes. Doctors reported that 68% of patients arrived at appointments better prepared and more engaged with their treatment plan.

By rethinking how patients interact with their treatment, the Invisalign Dashboard turned a complex medical process into a simple and empowering experience. It helped patients not only see their progress but also feel proud of it every step of the way.
