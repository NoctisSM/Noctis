Invisalign (2022)

Challenge
Once treatment began, many Invisalign patients found it difficult to stay connected to their progress. They received aligners, followed instructions, and attended appointments, but often lacked a clear sense of how far they had come or what was next. Without an easy way to visualize progress, track wear time, or communicate with their doctor, motivation tended to fade over time. The existing experience felt fragmented. Patients had to rely on multiple emails, manual reminders, or third-party apps to stay on track. The challenge was to design a central space that could keep patients engaged and informed throughout their smile journey.

Solution
The idea behind the new Invisalign Patient Dashboard was to create a single, personalized hub that brought clarity and encouragement to every stage of treatment. The dashboard became a digital companion, helping patients see their journey unfold in real time. The new experience allowed patients to:

View their treatment progress at a glance, including completed and upcoming aligner stages.

Receive personalized reminders for aligner changes, check-ins, and retainer care.

Access educational tips and support resources tailored to their phase of treatment.

Stay connected with their doctor through messages and virtual updates when needed.

The layout balanced structure and freedom. It guided patients through their treatment without ever feeling restrictive, making the process feel transparent, motivating, and personal.

Impact
The dashboard transformed how patients experienced Invisalign treatment. What once felt like a hidden process became an interactive and encouraging journey. Early results and feedback showed several improvements:

Patients felt more informed and confident about their progress.

Adherence to aligner schedules increased, reducing treatment delays.

Doctors reported that patients arrived at check-ins better prepared and more engaged.

By rethinking how patients interact with their treatment, the Invisalign Dashboard turned a complex medical process into a simple and empowering experience. It helped patients not only see their progress but also feel proud of it every step of the way.
