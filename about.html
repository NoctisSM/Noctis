About

I’m Samhith — a product designer focused on building thoughtful, human-centered systems.

My work is driven by curiosity about how people move through everyday experiences and where design can make those experiences clearer, fairer, and more effective. Growing up around diverse communities shaped how I approach problem-solving: by listening closely, identifying shared patterns, and designing with real-world context in mind.

Before design, I explored psychology research, filmmaking, and digital media, which sharpened how I understand users, tell stories, and translate insight into action. Today, I focus on simplifying complex problems through scalable design systems, creative tools, and emerging technologies.

I’ve worked with teams at Porsche and Align Technology, designing scalable systems and simplifying complex workflows. I specialize in emerging technology, creative tools, and design systems.
